ABOUT ME

Who am I?

Hi! How are you? My name is Roisin Clear, I'm 20 years old, and I'm currently at University studying Media, Journalism and Publishing. I'm an aspiring journalist/presenter and am a disabled model. I'm also an avid user of social media (aren't we all!) and I love to write and love photography. I've always been drawn to the creative subjects as I like the idea of putting my stamp on something, I also like that the creative subjects, such as writing or photography give you the scope to experiment and expand. Part of the reason I chose my university course is that we get to learn how to use lots of different creative mediums which will be valuable for my future career, anyway, enough, about me! Hope you enjoy!
